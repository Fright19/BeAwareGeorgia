In Cobb County, 1.1% of the housing units were built before 1940

and 12.5% were built between 1940 and 1969

The median year a housing unit was built in Cobb County is 1989 (1978 for the U.S. as a whole).

Approximately 14-30% of homes were made prior to 1974. In other words, before the Toxic Substances Act

Source: U.S. Census Bureau, American Community Survey 5-year estimates, PolicyMap

Gentrification can displace residents, leaving their only option to rent homes that are cheap but of lesser quality

Georgia in particular has a litany of programs by their Historic Division

That aid residents, providing them with safe and affordable housing
